In the first period of the timeframe, the number of computers connected to ZiyoNet(a network aimed at students and teachers to provide a secure internet connection) in Tashkent was larger than in any other region in Uzbekistan. As ZiyoNet originated in 2006, the reason is unlikely to be a pilot project; the large number does not mean the government experimented before expanding the project. The main reason can be infrastructure readiness. However, it might heavily impact the computer literacy of students across the country over the period.
